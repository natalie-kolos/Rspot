Become a Relationspot Member!

We are an inclusive service for matchmaking, coaching, and community building for people of all abilities who have so much to offer and find it challenging to make connections. Whether you’re looking for love or friendship, our Relationspot team is here to support you every step of the way.

Membership at Relationspot gives you access to our social events, community classes, and our team of real-life, Relationspot Certified Coaches who introduce you to like-minded people who want to connect with YOU.
